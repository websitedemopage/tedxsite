ABOUT TEDxMITS
At TEDxMITS, we hope to bring together ideas and spirits that have resolved to crush the existing cliches in their own unorthodox ways. This is where the roads converge for the dreamer, the queer, the atypical, the mavericks, the abberant and the square pegs in the round holes.
 
 
 
ABOUT OUR THEME
Void. Valley. Barrier. The reasons might be many to not call that stranger, a brother. As we plunge head first into the pool of dizzying chaos of the universe, we are threatened not just by the enemies of fire and water but will be wrecked by the "the usual", "the regular", "the conservative" and ultimately "the cliche". But the horizon of hope is still bright. A skyward fall of realisations is all that it takes to realise that a stranger is a friend that you havent met yet.

ABOUT TED
TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading, usually in the form of short, powerful talks (18 minutes or fewer) delivered by today’s leading thinkers and doers. TED’s open and free initiatives for spreading ideas include TED.com, where new TED Talk videos are posted daily; the Open Translation Project, which provides subtitles and interactive transcripts as well as translations from thousands of volunteers worldwide.
ABOUT TEDx
In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized ( Subject to certain rules and regulations.).

Sharan Nair

"All the world's a stage and all the men and women merely comedians" – Unknown.
 
Evolution has bestowed Homo Sapiens with a unique ability - to crack jokes and enjoy them. This Kochi based content creator is then probably at the helm of it. He has tickled the funny bones of Indians with his Netflix series "Granny Reacts " . Having shot to fame with his Instagram prank videos, Sharan hit the jackpot when he collaborated with Netflix and the rest is history. What makes him so endearing is that he is like anyone of us, any time, any day- that typical youth who loves to wake up late, drink 'chai', go for long walks and listen to his parents rant about how spoilt he is :-P.

Wg Cdr M A Afraz (Retd)

"We may not know them all, but we owe them all".

Our nation owes a debt to the fallen valiant soldiers that we can never fully repay. The life and legacy of the martyrs gets buried under the barrage of news and we seldom remember the trauma undergone by the loved ones.
This retired Indian Air Force officer from Bangalore is doing his bit to resurrect the fallen heroes in the virtual space. Founder of Honourpoint, an online memorial for all the martyrs of our armed forces since Independence, his experiences have been both overwhelming and heart touching.
We are honoured and proud to have former Wing Commander M A Afraz on board TEDxMITS, where we provoke conversations that matter.

Teena Kondody

 
A flea market, a workshop for kids, or an obscured corner- you can catch her almost everywhere, silently working away with what might seem like junk- plastic bottles, dry leaves or old discs. She has her way with hands to transform all these bric-a-bracs to breathtaking pieces of art. The works of this art teacher from Manganam, are a gentle reminder, that art cannot be simply confined to something that is physically existing, it is all about returning to our roots and redefining our inner cosmic energy. Before her current stint as an art instructor, she was on a silent crusade through the slums of Chennai as a facilitator of needs, before going on to work in the blind schools of Olassa. These astounding transitions have left profound impressions on this 27 year old and she hopes to be the candle that spreads light and the mirror that reflects it.

Team Omm

 
If sarcasm was a superpower, then OMM (Offensive’nt malayalam memes) is definitely a bandwagon of superheroes. This batallion of youngsters under the veil of anonymity churns out the right amount of humour-laced take on socially relevant issues. What makes OMM stand out in the ocean of meme pages strewn across social media is that their memes tread on the fine line between sarcasm and insensitivity with grace. In a room where people unanimously maintain a conspiracy of silence, a single voice of truth sounds like a pistol shot and these youngsters believe that genuine content is as mighty as one.

Sleeba Paul


Artificial intelligence in the simplest of terms is "magic". We have unforeseen monumental transitions at an unprecedented rate, redefining our present, future and even our past Here is Sleeba Paul, a machine learning engineer, who attempts to write the 5th Gospel employing the power of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. This humble attempt tries to debunk the twisted dogmas used by people mislead the masses rather than sow seeds of love and brotherhood, while interpreting the Holy Texts of different religions Among his commendable projects, the jewel on his crown remains the Microsoft funded project, Auria Kathi -"World's first Artificial Intelligence Poet Artist" Auria Kathi-an anagram for "Al Haiku Art", lives in the "cloud", conjures up images, writes poems and maintains an Instagram account. In fact we can soon find her magic unfolding at Florence Biennale, Italy.

Sabareesh Narayanan


Better known as "standup sabari", this 22 year old Kochite has multiple hats and just one head-advertising consultant, filmmaker and entrepreneur. He began his career by giving opening acts for stalwarts like Praveen Kumar and Sorabh Pant and is now a regular at Comedy Lounge. Fuelled by sarcasm, caffeine and everything fine, he has carved a niche for himself in the comedy space of Kerala. Juggling the fine act of script and improvisation he claims, is his key to "laughter". Is infectious energy, keen observation and an eye for detail makes him one of the most sought after standup comedians in Kerala.
